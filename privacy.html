Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026 · Applies to humantype.sherhn.space

1. No data collection

HumanType does not collect, store, or process any personal data. All text processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. No text is ever sent to a server.

2. Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts of any kind. There is no advertising on HumanType.

3. Third-party services

HumanType does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log standard HTTP requests according to their own privacy policy.

4. Data security

The website is served over HTTPS. Since no user data is stored, there is no risk of data breach through our infrastructure.

5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact sherhn.inc@gmail.com.
